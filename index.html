About ShieldScan Security

We're a South African cybersecurity company with one mission: make enterprise-level website security accessible to every business, not just the big ones.

Our automated scanning engine checks for the vulnerabilities that attackers actually exploit — misconfigured headers, weak SSL, exposed admin panels, outdated CMS versions, and OWASP Top 10 risks — giving you clear, actionable results without the jargon.

We built ShieldScan because we watched too many local businesses get blindsided by breaches that were completely preventable. Prevention is always cheaper than recovery.
